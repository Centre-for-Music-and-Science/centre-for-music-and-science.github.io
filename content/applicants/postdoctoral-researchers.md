We welcome enquiries from prospective postdoctoral researchers interested in working at the Centre for Music and Science. Opportunities depend on available funding and current research priorities.

For more information about postdoctoral research opportunities at the CMS, please contact the CMS Director, Peter Harrison, at [pmch2@cam.ac.uk](mailto:pmch2@cam.ac.uk).

You may also wish to check the University of Cambridge [job vacancies page](https://www.jobs.cam.ac.uk/) for any current openings.
