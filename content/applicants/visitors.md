We welcome academic visitors to the CMS through the Faculty's [Academic Visitors scheme](https://www.mus.cam.ac.uk/research/academic-visitors-information). Visits would typically last for an academic term or longer, with the visitor proposing an academic research project that they plan to conduct during their stay. Visitors are welcome to attend the CMS seminars and other meetings, as well as using facilities such as office space, libraries, and computers.

Academic visits are normally self-funded, but there are also various funding schemes that can support such visits. Examples include:

- [Leverhulme Trust Visiting Professorships](https://www.leverhulme.ac.uk/sites/default/files/VP_guidance_2024.pdf)
- [British Academy Global Professorships](https://www.thebritishacademy.ac.uk/funding/global-professorships/) (for mid-career and senior researchers)
