## Multimodal Organ Performance
Katelyn Emerson is applying similar methodologies to classical organ performances. She is building a comprehensive dataset of organ performances by world-class musicians at two Cambridge College chapels. Her data collection encompasses a wide spectrum of measurements, including audio, video, MIDI data, questionnaires, and structured interviews. This rich dataset will inform various research questions about performance practice, particularly examining the complex interplay between performer, instrument, and venue.
