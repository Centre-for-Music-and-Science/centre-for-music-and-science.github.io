This project follows up evidence that even brief spontaneous musical interaction can increase affiliative markers in later conversation.
The team uses dual-EEG (hyperscanning) to examine how non-experts' neural activity is modulated while making music together, and to identify shared cognitive processes underlying spontaneous interaction in speech and music.
