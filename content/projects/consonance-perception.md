title: "Consonance Perception Across Cultures"
theme: "music-cognition"
summary: "Investigating how timbral, psychoacoustic, and cultural factors shape consonance preferences worldwide."
image: ""
---

This project investigates the perceptual and cultural foundations of musical consonance. Using cross-cultural experiments and computational modelling, we disentangle the contributions of psychoacoustic mechanisms (such as roughness and harmonicity) from culturally learned preferences. Our findings suggest that timbral properties of instruments play a key role in shaping consonance perception and may help explain the origins of musical scales.
