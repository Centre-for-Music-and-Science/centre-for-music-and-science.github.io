This project creates rich multimodal datasets capturing expert organ performances, combining audio recordings with MIDI data, video, and performance metadata. By studying how organists interpret scores through rubato, articulation, and registration choices, we gain insight into the embodied and cognitive processes underlying expert musical performance.
