---
title: "New paper: Consonance in the carillon"
date: 2024-08-21
summary: "New paper in the Journal of the Acoustical Society of America exploring how the carillon bell's frequency spectrum alters consonance perception."
---

Our new paper entitled 'Consonance in the carillon' has just been published in the Journal of the Acoustical Society of America. This paper grew out of an undergraduate dissertation by James MacConnachie. The paper explores an interesting phenomenon whereby the idiosyncratic frequency spectrum of the carillon bell alters the usual hierarchy of consonance and dissonance.
