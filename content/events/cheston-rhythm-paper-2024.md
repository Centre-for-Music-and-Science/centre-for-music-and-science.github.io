We are excited to share our new paper appearing in the journal Royal Society Open Science, entitled "Rhythmic Qualities of Jazz Improvisation Predict Performer Identity and Style in Source-Separated Audio Recordings". This was completed by Huw Cheston during his PhD at the CMS, and builds from two earlier publications appearing in Transactions of the International Society for Music Information Retrieval.
